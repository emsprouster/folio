Emma Sprouster
Design Director
BNE
QLD, AUS

Emma is an Australian Design Director specialising in brand identity, visual systems, typography, and art direction; with 11 years of experience working across corporate, property, hospitality, and arts and cultural sectors. Recognised as an AGDA Emerging Designer of the Year (2020, Distinction); she is currently based in Brisbane (Meanjin), Australia and working at Studio ODEA, delivering brands for some of Australia's largest institutional property and infrastructure clients.

She actively works at the intersection of traditional design craft and emerging technology, building sophisticated design systems with comprehensive and cohesive digital, print and environmental presences. Her practice is grounded in empathy, constantly working in service of humanity.

Please email if you would like to discuss a potential project or request a digital portfolio. I love receiving interesting emails.
